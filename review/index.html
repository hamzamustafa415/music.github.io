    Best Albums Review

    Strong
    Jekk

    France #pop

    Simply an amazing album, able to seduce starting the first notes, that will stay in the memories for long. With Strong, Jekk proves that they're able to reach the top, by delivering a magic & mesmerizing #pop!

    Blue Electric Eyes
    Neon Niteclub

    USA #disco #funk

    On 'Blue Electric Eyes', Neon NiteClub orchestrates a fruitful encounter between a #nostalgic #80s #disco feeling and some heavy #funk basslines created to set fire to the dancefloor!

    Sun-drenched Vol. 1
    (Her Summer Mixtape)
    The Ambient Light

    US #dreampop

    Filled with dreamy sounds that feel like summer and inspired by 90's lofi hip-hop beats, 'Sun-drenched Vol. 1 (Her Summer Mixtape)' is a true #dreampop gem from The Ambient Light that you won't get bored too soon!

    Fragments: Disclosure
    So Far As i Know

    Russia #postrock

    With 'Fragments: Disclosure', So Far As I Know deliver a majestic #postrock monument, where atmospheric guitar riffs merge with musical influences from all around the world, taking you on a timeless journey!

    Tall Pines & Tangled Vines
    The Rabbitts

    Canada #folk

    An incredible #folk effort called 'Tall Pines & Tangled Vines' in which Canadian folk duo The Rabbits evocate their gentle, fun and soothing folk music universe that they have been working on since their debut EP 'Avocado Ginger Tree'!

    Ghosts
    LukHash

    UK #chiptune #electropop

    If it's no news that LukHash is an absolute master of the #chiptune genre, his album 'Ghosts' proves that he's still got a lot of creativity to express! From his electronic mechanics, he twists his #chiptune melodies with #dubstep or #drumandbass influences, all for our ultimate pleasure!

    Excuses For The Common Man
    Heifervescent

    UK #indierock

    Touching subjects like love, cats and sci-fi, 'Excuses for the Common Man' was never meant to be a traditional #indierock album. It's a well-crafted balance between vintage sounds from the mid-60s & contemporary #indie, with a single watchword: originality! 

    Two
    Saint Anyway

    USA #folk

    Saint Anyway's album 'Two' gathers all the very best from traditional country #folk music: high-energized #banjo riffs, #happy vocals singing thoughtful songs and cheerful #strings or #harmonicas melodies crafted to brighten up your day!
