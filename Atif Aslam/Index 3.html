    Personal Detail

    Name: Muhammad Atif Aslam

    NickName: Aadee

    Profession: Singer,songwriter,Actor

    Age: 35 Years Old

    Bate of Birth: 12 March 1983

    BirthPlace: Wazirabad Pakistan

    Nationality: Pakistani

    Religion: Islam

    Career Achievments

    'Aadat' from his first album Jal Pari won International Awards.

    Two songs 'Doorie' and 'Maula' were selected in 2010 for the soundtrack of the Spanish Beauty, the Hindi version of the Spanish film.

    Indus Movie Award in the titles: Best Lyrics, Best Song, and Best Composition for his song 'Aadat'.

    Best Playback Singer and Best Debut Singer by Sahara Sangeet Awards.

    His two Albums: Jal Pari and Doorie received The Best Album Award from The Lux Style Award.

    He has received other several awards for his music.
