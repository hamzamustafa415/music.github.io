    Career Achievments

    In his brief career of almost seven to eight years, Ali Zafar has gained many commercial successes, for all that reasons Ali Zafar is considered to be the 2nd most popular celebrity according to Facebook trends and stats. Some of his below mentioned remarkable achievements have earned him enormous respect that adds to his massive fan following across the globe.

    Ali Zafar holds the record of being the 3rd most googled musician in Indo-Pak region leaving A.R Rehman behind.

    Ali has managed to win almost all major awards starting from the Best Album in 2003 to the Best Male Artist at MTV Awards in 2009.

    He was also awarded for Best Pop Artist and Best Music Album by Indus Music Awards, Lux Style Awards and Asian Bollywood Music Awards.
